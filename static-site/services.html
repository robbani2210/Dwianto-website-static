Our Services

Integrated Advisory Framework

We combine financial modeling, governance structuring, regulatory navigation, and negotiation strategy into one disciplined advisory approach for cross-border transactions.

Service 01

Mergers & Acquisitions

Buy-side and sell-side advisory for cross-border M&A transactions involving Indonesian companies and foreign investors. We provide end-to-end support from mandate origination through transaction close.

What We Deliver

  • — Target identification and preliminary screening
  • — Financial and legal due diligence coordination
  • — Transaction structure optimization
  • — Negotiation support and term sheet drafting
  • — Regulatory approval coordination (BKPM, OJK, KPPU)
  • — Post-merger integration planning

Buy-Side Advisory

We represent foreign investors seeking acquisition targets in Indonesia — managing the full process from initial screening through SPA execution.

Sell-Side Advisory

We prepare Indonesian businesses for sale, identifying appropriate acquirers and managing the process to maximize transaction value.

Service 02

Cross-Border Investment Structuring

Strategic market entry solutions for foreign investors entering Indonesia through direct investment. We design and implement optimal investment structures that comply with Indonesian foreign investment law while protecting investor interests.

Key Services

  • — PT PMA establishment and licensing
  • — Ownership structure optimization
  • — Capital injection and repatriation planning
  • — Joint venture structuring with Indonesian partners
  • — Negative Investment List (DNI) navigation
  • — OSS (Online Single Submission) licensing support

Structuring Foreign Investment for Long-Term Success

Indonesia's investment framework offers significant opportunities, but the path from investment decision to operational business requires precise structural engineering. A well-structured market entry defines your operational flexibility, tax efficiency, and exit options for years to come.

Minimum Investment Threshold · IDR 10 Billion per KBLI

Service 03

Legal & Financial Compliance

Governance structuring and regulatory compliance advisory for investor-backed Indonesian companies. We help Indonesian businesses and their foreign investors build the governance infrastructure required to meet institutional standards.

Scope of Services

  • — Corporate governance framework design
  • — Regulatory compliance audit and gap analysis
  • — BPKM investment reporting (LKPM)
  • — Tax compliance and transfer pricing advisory
  • — Employment law compliance (BPJS, manpower regulations)
  • — Investor-ready financial reporting frameworks

Legal Compliance

Continuous monitoring and management of regulatory obligations under Indonesian company law, investment regulations, and sector-specific requirements.

Governance Structuring

Board structure design, shareholder agreement drafting, and management reporting frameworks aligned with international institutional standards.
