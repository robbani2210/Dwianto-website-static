About

Built for the Intersection of Global Capital and Indonesian Growth

Dwianto Capital Advisory is an independent strategic advisory firm with a single focus: helping foreign investors and Indonesian businesses navigate the intersection of capital, regulation, and commercial opportunity in the Indonesian market.

A Different Kind of Advisory

Unlike large generalist firms, we work on a select number of mandates at any given time. That means full partner-level attention to every transaction — from initial structuring through final close. We don't hand files to junior associates.

Our Unique Position

We operate at the intersection of legal expertise, financial structuring, and regulatory intelligence. This integrated capability allows us to identify and resolve transaction risks before they become deal-breaking issues.

The Challenge

Why Indonesia Demands Specialized Advisory

The Problem

Indonesia offers exceptional growth potential, but foreign investors consistently face the same barriers: fragmented regulatory requirements, opaque local ownership rules, and limited access to investment-ready counterparties. Local businesses, meanwhile, lack the governance frameworks and capital structures required to attract sophisticated institutional investors.

Our Solution

We solve both sides of this equation simultaneously. We prepare Indonesian companies to meet international investor standards while providing foreign capital with structured, de-risked entry pathways. Our integrated framework — combining legal expertise, financial modeling, and regulatory navigation — reduces friction and increases transaction certainty.
